About Us

About Southern Heat Exchangers

Southern Heat Exchangers, a partnership-based firm established in 1990, specializes in manufacturing and supplying high-quality components for diverse engineering and mechanical applications, including custom-bent copper tubes, oil coolers, heat exchangers, and precision CNC-machined components.

  • Uses superior quality materials sourced from trusted and reliable vendors.
  • Nationwide market presence with a strong client base.
  • Precise manufacturing and cost-effective solutions for better reliability.
“Led by Managing Partner Mr. P. Muruganantham, who actively oversees production, ensures strict checks, and continuously updates team knowledge to align with evolving market requirements.”

Result-oriented manpower with a healthy work environment, capable of handling heavy assignments within deadlines, supported by regular motivational and capability-enhancement sessions.
